Pumpkin Pie Spice

Now that fall has arrived, it's time to create batches of one of the most important spice mix of the season! Use these for any pumpkin spice-related recipe!
  • 2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 tablespoon ground ginger
  • 1 teaspoon nutmeg
  • ½ teaspoon ground cloves
  • ½ teaspoon allspice
